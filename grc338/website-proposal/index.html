Final project proposal

Introduction

Blue Star Fruit Stationary

This is a site for customers to browse different types of stationary. There will be categories of planners, pencils, notebooks, among other things. Through the site people can view different items that are in stock and purchase said item. They can also visit the physical location since there will be a page that details the address

Target audience

The target audience is for younger people, specifically any student ranging from elementary to even college. The stye of the products will be more kawaii and appeals to those that are attracted to that aesthetic.

The primary task is of this site is an online shop. Customers can purchase directly on it instead of going into an actual store. It will also let customers know about this business so that they can be redirected to the physical location if they live in the area. They can also contact for any questions regarding the products.
